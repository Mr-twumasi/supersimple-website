About Us

Word Liberty Chapel International is a growing family of believers committed to worshipping God, spreading the Gospel, and building lives that shine with purpose and love. We believe in the transforming power of God’s Word, lived out through faith, service, and community.

Our Mission

To preach and teach sound biblical truth with relevance and clarity. To create venues of worship where God’s presence is experienced. To serve our local community, showing Christ’s love in action. To raise and mentor leaders who will carry the torch for future generations.

Our Vision

To see transformed lives, strengthened communities, and a church that impacts city, nation, and generation with hope, faith, and compassion.

Our Core Beliefs

  • The Bible is God’s Word, sufficient for life and faith.
  • Jesus Christ is Lord, Savior, and the way to salvation.
  • The Holy Spirit empowers, guides, and comforts.
  • Discipleship, prayer, worship, and fellowship are central to our life together.
  • Serving others in love is our expression of faith.
